Performance Marketing

We help brands leverage different performance marketing services such as CPI, CPL, CPA, and CPS to achieve their business objectives. By developing customized strategies, implementing advanced targeting techniques, and continuously optimizing campaigns for maximum performance.

We also work with some of the largest ad networks in the world who are partners in delivering good quality leads.

Cost Per Install (CPI):

We drive app installations through targeted advertising across various channels such as mobile ad networks, social media platforms, and app stores for our brands. We use advanced targeting techniques to reach users most likely to install the app, optimize ad creatives and messaging to increase conversion rates, and continuously monitor and optimize campaigns.

Cost Per Lead (CPL):

We are experts in CPA campaigns and help brands drive conversions and customer acquisitions through multiple DSPs, inhouse inventories, Google & Facebook Ads and with the help of our ad network partners.

Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):

We work closely with brands to develop comprehensive marketing strategies that are aligned with their business objectives and target audience preferences. This includes identifying the most effective channels, communication strategies, and tactics to reach and engage their target audience effectively.

Cost Per Sale (CPS):

Our expert CPS team helps brands drive sales and revenue through affiliate marketing programs, partner networks, and referral campaigns.
