Social Media & Influencer Marketing

We help brands in creating and sharing content on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok to connect with target audiences, build brand awareness, and foster engagement.

We have partnered with 2000+ influential individuals, celebrities, or content creators on social media platforms to promote brands or products to their followers.

How do we do?

Strategy Development

We first brainstorm with brands to develop customized social media and influencer marketing strategies aligned with their business goals, target audience, and budget. This includes identifying the most effective social media platforms, selecting the right influencers, and outlining key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success.

Influencer Identification and Outreach

We have more than 2000+ creators and if needed our experts reach out to onboard any specific influencer, celebrities.

Content Creation and Management

We collaborate with influencers to develop engaging and authentic content that showcases brands' products or services. Once we are convinced with the content we share that with our brands for their approval before we start spreading them on different social media platforms.

Campaign Execution and Monitoring

our team is thoroughly involved in scheduling content, tracking campaign performance, responding to comments and messages, and adjusting strategies based on real-time feedback and data insights.

Reporting

We provide a campaign reports with all possible data points to understand the real benefits of the campaigns.
