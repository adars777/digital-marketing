Media Planning and Buying

Our expert media planning and buying team work extensively on behalf of our clients to strategize the plan, negotiate, and purchase advertising space across various media channels to reach a target audience effectively.

This includes traditional channels such as television, radio, print, and outdoor advertising, as well as digital channels such as display ads, video ads, social media ads, and programmatic advertising.

Our Systematic Approach

Audience Analysis

We conduct in-depth research to understand the target audience's demographics, interests, behaviors, and media consumption habits.

Channel Selection

Based on audience insights and campaign objectives, we then select the most appropriate media channels and platforms to reach the target audience effectively.

Budget Allocation

We allocate brand’s advertising budgets strategically, considering factors such as reach, frequency, and cost per impression (CPM).

Negotiation and Buying

On behalf of our clients, we negotiate with media vendors to secure the best possible rates and placements for the brand's advertising campaigns.

Campaign Execution

We manage end to end campaign execution, including scheduling ad placements, tracking performance metrics, and optimizing campaign delivery.

Reporting and Analysis

We provide extensive reports to our clients to understand the campaign performance.
